About Senator Rodriguez

Born and raised in Colorado, Senator Maria Rodriguez has dedicated her life to public service. As a former teacher, small business owner, and community organizer, she understands the challenges facing everyday Coloradans.

Since taking office, Senator Rodriguez has been a champion for working families, fighting to protect our public lands, expand healthcare access, and build a clean energy economy that creates good-paying jobs.
